I'm a PhD candidate working on multimodal AI.

I completed my Ph.D. at the University of Modena and Reggio Emilia, conducting my research at AImageLab, co-advised by Prof. Rita Cucchiara, Prof.Lorenzo Baraldi and Prof. Marcella Cornia. I did a 6-month internship at Amazon in London during my PhD.

At AImageLab, I worked on image captioning and evaluation and multimodal architectures. Nowadays, I am working on Multimodal LLMs, Retrieval-augmentation for multimodal models ,and hallucination.
